Harvard College Children's Stories

Harvard College Children's Stories integrates students' interests in creative writing, drawing, and foreign languages, coordinating their creative efforts for the benefit of under-served children abroad and in the local Boston community. It is our hope that by presenting each child with a book written, illustrated, and bound especially for him or her, we will inspire a love of reading. During the semester, we also do outreach in engaging local children to develop an appreciation for cross-cultural connection and literacy.
