About HCCS!


What We Do!

Harvard College Children's Stories is a non-profit student-run group dedicated to supporting child literacy around the globe. We are a recognized Harvard student organization.

We work with non-profit organizations to provide custom-designed children's books for kids in need. In the past, we've worked with Neipu Elementary School, National Institutes of Health, Rady Children's Hospitals, and more! Additionally, we also work on special projects, like our COVID-19 Anthology, or our series of Fireside Chats with fantastic children's book authors.

How to Join!

We consist of illustrators and authors who are passionate about writing fascinating stories that any child can treasure. Check out our previously published books under the "Books" tab to see what we have been working on! Our group is open to all Harvard undergraduates - we welcome any and all levels of experience! We recruit in the fall semester, so email us to be added to the email list, or look out for notifications of how and when to apply. We also have a continued partnership with the Massachusetts College of Arts and Design.

Any questions? Contact us at harvard.childrens.stories@gmail.com
